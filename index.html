About Us

Co-founders and member-owners

Dos Manos means "Both Hands", but it's also short for Dos Hermanos, or "Two Brothers"

Francisco Jimenez

Originally from Costa Rica, Francisco combines his experience of Latin American cuisine with his love of food from all over, which he has been preparing for over 30 years. Among his specialty skills is producing his own tropical vinegars such as banana, plantain, jackfruit, pineapple, and many others.

Matt Senate

Matt fell in love with cooking at an early age. As a Californian, he was eventually introduced to the creative world of vegetarian and vegan food culture. He cooks animal products too, but his skills extend from learning to do more with less and to celebrate the whole spectrum of food and flavor.
